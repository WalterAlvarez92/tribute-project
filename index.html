Chester Bennington

03/20/1976 - 07/20/2017

Chester was a lead vocalist and was about to give up on music all together before moving to LA and and auditioning for the band Linkin Park. On October 24th, 2000, Linkin Park relelased their first album, Hybrid Theory which seen immediate success selling about 10 million copies in the US alone. Linkin Park was most know for combining a mix of rock & hiphop, trying to close the gap on music genres they experimented and drew many different audiences to their music.

In Chesters early life he struggled with drug addiction binging on alcohol and methemphetamine, At one point dropping down to 110 lbs. Chester was also sexually abused at the age of 7 up until he was 13, fearful that no one would beleive him he remained silent and it ultimately led to the drug addictions and was able to express himself within his music. Music was his way of coping with his past experiences and he beleived that he was able to turn the negative events into art.
